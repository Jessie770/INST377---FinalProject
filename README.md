# Welcome to the Global Holidays Website!

Here, you will be able to lookup 100+ countries Here you will be able to choose over a 100+ countries and select one you are interested in learning more about. Then you would enter in a year ranging between 1980 to 2075 and from there, learn more about what holidays are celebrated there,the date it occured in that specfic year, the local name of the holiday, what it's called in English, and the type of holiday it is. Below the holiday table, you'll see a list of additional information about the country, including the languages spoken there, the currency they use, and the location of the capital city on a map.

Types of holidays include:

- Public

- Bank (Bank holiday, banks and offices are closed)

- School (School holiday, schools are closed)

- Authorities (Authorities are closed)

- Optional (Majority of people take a day off)

- Observance (Optional festivity, no paid day off)
